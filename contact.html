Common Questions

Before You
Reach Out

What's your minimum order?

There isn't one set in stone. We regularly do runs as small as 10–15 pieces for artists and small projects. Just ask — we'll figure it out together.

Do you do the design too?

Yes. We can take your rough idea, reference images, or a full brief and turn it into print-ready artwork. Design is a core part of what we do.

What file formats do you need?

If you have finished artwork, we prefer vector files (AI, EPS, PDF). High-res PNGs or PSDs work too. Not sure? Just send what you have and we'll tell you what we need.

Do you ship?

Yes — we ship nationwide. Local pickup and delivery in the Minneapolis area is also available. Posters are rolled and tubed; apparel is folded and boxed with care.
