The Hand Print

Ink, intent,
and a lot of
elbow grease.

The Hand Print Shop started with a simple belief: good design should live in the physical world. Not just on a screen. Not just as a file. As something you can hold, hang, wear, and feel.

We're a boutique print shop and design studio planted firmly in Northeast Minneapolis — one of the most creatively alive neighborhoods in the country. We work with musicians, activists, small businesses, artists, and anyone else with something real to say.

Every project that comes through our door gets the same thing: full attention, honest craft, and a genuine desire to make something worth keeping. We don't do cookie-cutter. We don't do rush-it-out. We pull every screen with intention.

Northeast Minneapolis is home for a reason. This neighborhood has always been a place where makers make things — where the old and new coexist, where warehouses become studios and alleys become galleries. We're part of that tradition, and we take it seriously.

What We Stand For

Principles we
print by

01

Craft Over Speed

We'd rather take an extra day and get it right than rush a run that doesn't hold up. Quality isn't a selling point — it's the baseline. Every print leaves here looking exactly how it should.

02

Design That Means Something

We're not a template shop. Our graphic design work starts from scratch, driven by the client's vision and sharpened by our own aesthetic instincts. We make things that earn their place in the world.

03

Community First

NE Minneapolis isn't just an address. It's our people, our neighbors, our collaborators. We prioritize local artists, local bands, local causes — because the best work is made close to home.

04

Small Runs Welcome

You don't need to order 500 to work with us. We specialize in short-run, limited-edition printing for people who care about quality over quantity. The best art doesn't always need scale.

Why We Do It

Because the
work matters.

Prints outlast digital content. A poster on a wall, a shirt on your back, a bag in your hand — these things carry stories. We take that responsibility seriously, and we love it.
